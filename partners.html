Our Valued Partners

From Farm to Table

Our commitment to culinary excellence begins with our ingredients. We believe that the best dishes are made from the freshest, highest-quality produce, sourced from local farmers and artisans who share our passion. By supporting local communities, we not only ensure the quality of our food but also contribute to a sustainable future.

Green Valley Organics

Fresh Vegetables

Located just outside Bengaluru, Green Valley provides us with daily harvests of crisp, organic vegetables, ensuring every salad and side dish is bursting with natural flavor.

Malnad Dairy Cooperative

Artisanal Dairy

Our creamy paneer and rich yogurts come from the dedicated farmers of the Malnad region, known for their high-quality milk and traditional dairy practices.

Coastal Spice Merchants

Aromatic Spices

From the coastal belts of Karnataka, we source our authentic, hand-ground spices that form the heart and soul of our traditional Indian recipes.
